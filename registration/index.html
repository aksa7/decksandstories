What is Decks & Stories: Open Decks?

Open Decks is a DJ showcase and contest in Vilnius. You register online, we listen to your mix, select a few DJs for the event, record the sets and share them online so the community can discover you.

How it works

  1. Stage 1 – Online registration (until December 2)
    Fill in the registration form, write a short intro about yourself, share your social links and an up to 30-minute mix. We listen to every submission and select 5 DJs that best fit the sound and energy of the event.
  2. Stage 2 – Live night in Vilnius (December 11)
    The selected DJs play their sets at the venue on December 11, 18:00–00:00, Konstitucijos pr. 12, Vilnius. All performances are recorded by the Decks & Stories team.
  3. Stage 3 – Release on YouTube & Instagram (December 16)
    The recorded sets are released as a series on the Decks & Stories YouTube channel. On Instagram, each DJ gets a separate post with performance clips and tags for the partners. All posts go live at the same time.
  4. Stage 4 – Community voting (until December 20, 18:00)
    Voting happens on Instagram. The community supports their favorite DJ by liking the posts. The contest winner is the DJ whose post receives the most likes after December 20, 18:00.

About Us

Decks&Stories is a movement born in Kaunas, Lithuania – a platform where electronic music, identity and real stories meet.

Our mission is simple: grow internationally through genuine connections – collaborating and learning together instead of competing. We are building a reliable, free space where any DJ can present themselves, no matter their status or follower count.

Through digital episodes and events we aim to create honest, welcoming experiences where people feel seen, not judged – and where every set reveals who the DJ truly is. We want our nights and recordings to feel like chapters in a bigger, shared story.

Long term, Decks&Stories aims to host international events and feature DJs from as many countries as possible – we’re already at 14/195 worldwide and 8/44 in Europe, with Amsterdam as a vital next step in this journey.

Decks&Stories

Let’s build something together

Decks&Stories is a movement built on collaboration - between DJs, producers and people who have special spaces or ideas.

Have a venue or an event idea but not sure how to bring it to life? We can shape the whole experience together - from musical direction and storytelling to the full sound system, DJ setup and atmosphere of the night.

We can bring our own professional setup (sound system, decks, mixer, lights) or plug into your existing system, so the event feels not only loud, but meaningful.

If you already have a mix, story or unreleased demo, you can send it through our submit page. If you have a venue or concept in mind, reach out and let’s see if we can turn it into a Decks&Stories night.
